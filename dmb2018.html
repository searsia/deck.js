Targeted advertisements by Searsia

  • Based on the query, not on the person

  • Local (proxied) banners and images

  • No third party Javascript, Cookies, or web beacons

  • No HTTP referrer with query terms

Affiliate programs and networks

  • Connect to APIs of big affiliate programs (e.g. Amazon)

  • Local search in data from affiliate networks (e.g. ShareASale)

  • (Shows ads for charities if no results)
